Support

Gestures

  • Swipe up / down anywhere — set the BPM (0.1 steps, 20–300).
  • Drag the right-edge scale — jump straight to an absolute BPM.
  • Hold a second finger — fine mode: the swipe slows ~10× for decimal nudges.
  • Single tap — flip between half-time (×½) and double-time (×2).
  • Two-finger tap — cycle the beat style (Orbit, Ripple, Sweep, Pulse, Bare).
  • Shake — switch to a random theme.

VoiceOver

With VoiceOver on, focus the tempo and swipe up/down to change it; double-tap the mode row to flip half/double; use the rotor's Actions to change the beat style or theme.

FAQ

The haptic ticks aren't firing.

Haptics only work on a physical iPhone, not in the Simulator, and require that System Haptics are enabled in iOS Settings → Sounds & Haptics.

Where did my settings go?

Doppio stores everything on-device. Deleting the app clears your saved BPM, mode, beat style, and theme.

Does Doppio need internet?

No. It works fully offline and makes no network requests.
