Doppio Privacy Support

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Doppio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. The app makes no network requests.

What's stored on your device

Doppio remembers your preferences — your last BPM, the half/double mode, the chosen beat style, and the selected theme — using Apple's standard on-device settings storage (UserDefaults). This information never leaves your iPhone, is not accessible to us or anyone else, and is removed when you delete the app.

Data we collect

None. In Apple's terms, Doppio's privacy label is “Data Not Collected.”

Children's privacy

Doppio is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
